## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 First Time Login

1. **Receive Welcome Email**: Check your email for your login credentials
2. **Visit Member Portal**: Go to `{your-gym}.liyaqa.com/member/login`
3. **Enter Credentials**: Use your email and temporary password
4. **Change Password**: You'll be prompted to set a new password
5. **Complete Profile**: Fill in your personal information

### 1.2 Accessing the Member Portal

**Web Browser**:
- Desktop: `{your-gym}.liyaqa.com/member`
- Mobile: Same URL, mobile-optimized

**Mobile App**:
- Download from App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android)
- Search for "Liyaqa Member App"
- Login with your credentials

## 2. Managing Your Profile

### 2.1 Update Personal Information

1. Go to **Profile** from the menu
2. Click **Edit Profile**
3. Update your information:
   - Name
   - Phone number
   - Date of birth
   - Emergency contact
4. Click **Save Changes**

### 2.2 Change Password

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Security**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **Update Password**

### 2.3 Notification Preferences

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Notifications**
3. Choose your preferred channels:
   - Email
   - SMS
   - Push Notifications
   - WhatsApp
4. Select notification types you want to receive
5. Click **Save Preferences**

## 3. Viewing Your Subscription

### 3.1 Check Subscription Status

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. View your active subscription details:
   - Plan name
   - Start and end dates
   - Classes remaining this month
   - Guest passes available
   - Freeze days available
   - Auto-renewal status

### 3.2 View Membership Contract

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **View Contract**
3. Review your membership terms
4. Download PDF copy if needed

### 3.3 Freeze Your Membership

**When to Freeze**:
- Vacation or travel
- Injury or illness
- Temporary work commitments

**How to Freeze**:
1. Go to **Freeze** from the menu
2. View your available freeze days
3. Click **Request Freeze**
4. Select:
   - Start date
   - Number of days
   - Reason (optional)
5. Click **Submit Request**
6. Wait for approval (usually within 24 hours)

**Note**: Minimum freeze period is 7 days.

### 3.4 Upgrade or Downgrade Your Plan

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. View available plans and compare benefits
4. Select desired plan
5. Review proration (credit or additional charge)
6. Confirm plan change

### 3.5 Cancel Your Membership

1. Go to **Subscriptions**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Complete exit survey (helps us improve)
4. Review cancellation terms and fees
5. Confirm cancellation
6. Receive confirmation email

**Note**: Check your contract for notice period and cancellation fees.

## 4. Booking Classes

### 4.1 Browse Classes

1. Go to **Classes** or **Book a Class**
2. View upcoming classes for the next 7 days
3. Filter by:
   - Date
   - Class type (Yoga, Spinning, HIIT, etc.)
   - Trainer
   - Time of day

### 4.2 View Class Details

Click on any class to see:
- Class description
- Instructor information
- Duration
- Difficulty level
- Location/studio
- Available spots

### 4.3 Book a Class

1. Find your desired class
2. Click **Book Now**
3. Confirm booking details
4. Click **Confirm Booking**
5. Receive confirmation (email, SMS, or push notification)

**Reminders**:
- You'll receive reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before class
- Arrive 5-10 minutes early

### 4.4 Cancel a Booking

1. Go to **My Bookings**
2. Find the booking to cancel
3. Click **Cancel Booking**
4. Confirm cancellation

**Cancellation Policy**:
- Cancel at least 4 hours before class (may vary by gym)
- Late cancellations may incur penalties
- No-shows may result in booking restrictions

### 4.5 Waitlist

If a class is full:
1. Click **Join Waitlist**
2. You'll be notified if a spot opens up
3. You have 2 hours to confirm the booking

## 6. Payments & Invoices

### 6.1 View Invoices

1. Go to **Payments** or **Invoices**
2. View all invoices with status:
   - Pending (needs payment)
   - Paid
   - Overdue
   - Cancelled

### 6.2 Pay an Invoice

1. Go to **Payments**
2. Click on pending invoice
3. Click **Pay Now**
4. Select payment method:
   - Credit/Debit Card (PayTabs)
   - STC Pay
   - Bank Transfer (SADAD)
   - Buy Now Pay Later (Tamara)
5. Complete payment
6. Receive receipt via email

### 6.3 Manage Payment Methods

1. Go to **Settings** > **Payment Methods**
2. Add new card:
   - Click **Add Card**
   - Enter card details
   - Save securely
3. Set default payment method
4. Delete old/expired cards

### 6.4 View Payment History

1. Go to **Payments** > **History**
2. View all past payments
3. Download receipts as PDF

### 6.5 Wallet

**Top-Up Wallet**:
1. Go to **Wallet**
2. Click **Top-Up**
3. Enter amount
4. Select payment method
5. Confirm

**Use Wallet**:
- Wallet balance automatically applied to invoices
- View transaction history in Wallet tab

## 10. FAQs

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to you.

**Q: How do I check in at the gym?**
A: Show your QR code from the mobile app to reception, or tap your member card at the access control device.

**Q: When will I receive class reminders?**
A: You'll receive reminders 24 hours and 1 hour before the class starts.

**Q: What if I'm late cancelling a class?**
A: Late cancellations (within 4 hours of class start) may result in a penalty or booking restrictions.

**Q: How do I freeze my membership?**
A: Go to Subscriptions > Freeze, select dates and duration, and submit a request. Approval usually takes 24 hours.

**Q: Can I bring a guest?**
A: If your plan includes guest passes, you can bring a guest. Check your subscription details for guest pass availability.

**Q: How do I update my payment method?**
A: Go to Settings > Payment Methods, add a new card, and set it as default.

**Q: Where can I view my membership contract?**
A: Go to Subscriptions > View Contract to read or download your contract PDF.

**Q: How do I cancel my membership?**
A: Go to Subscriptions > Cancel Subscription. Review terms and complete the exit survey to proceed.

**Q: I didn't receive my booking confirmation. What should I do?**
A: Check your email spam folder. Also check your notifications in the app or member portal.
